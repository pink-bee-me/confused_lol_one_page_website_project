Introducing The Stellar Symposium...

About Us...

stellar
(adj.) exceptionally good; outstanding; having star-like qualities
symposium
( n.) reference to the celebrations in ancient Greece; specifically, the part of a banquet that takes place after a meal the meal, when drinking for pleasure is accompanied by music dancing ,recitals, or conversation.

Not just a name... Our name defines our ambition, goal, and purpose.


History Note:

The Greek symposium was a key Hellenic social instution.It was a forum for men to debate,plot boast, or simply revel with others. The symposium was held to celebrate special occasions ,such as athletic victories and poetic contests.The symposium was a source of pride for all.

OUR AMBITION:

  • to offer outstanding points of interest to the community
  • to deliver an exceptionally good quality experience; from the time you enter, to the food, drink, music, visual asthetics, auditory stimulus, total atmosphere, to departure
  • atmosphere of inspirational beauty

OUR GOAL:

  • to strengthen community spirit
  • to let people experience other cultures, forms of art, world views, and new technologies right here in the local area
  • provide a unique forum to share and discuss ideas
  • encourage social interaction in a "real" social setting

OUR PURPOSE:

  • To encourage people to engage with each other in an environment that is both intellectual and recreational.
  • To replicate, albeit with a much more modern twist, the symposium atmosphere of Ancient Greece and Rome, when men like Plato would debate, discuss philosophy, and share ideas.

What You Can Expect From Us:

  • An upscale experience,where people can exchange ideas, that encourages an open mind and open heart, in a fun, stress free space.
  • A cool place to "hang out", eat, drink, or just sit back, observe and relax.
  • Activities: Play darts, dance, listen to music.
  • We bring in art exhibits, live musicians, and guest speakers too!
  • We even will take suggestions for future monthly features.
  • An inviting atmosphere including: comfortable seating, buffet food bar (prepared by in-house gourmet chef), soft lighting, in door waterfall and coy pond, and multiple seating areas, and themed conference style rooms for private parties and business meetings available for booking
  • dance floor, stage, dart area, billiard area
  • area for meditation and peaceful solitude

The Stellar Symposium ...a new way to unwind and broaden your horizons.

Exposure to Music, Fine Art, Literary Works, New Technology and Cultural Diversity is an excellent way accomplish this goal.
