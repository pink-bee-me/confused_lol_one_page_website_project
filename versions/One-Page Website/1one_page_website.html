Introducing The Stellar Symposium...

About Us...

It's not just a name ...it defines our purpose, our goals and our ambitions.

stellar
(adj.) relating to a star or stars.
informal
featuring or having the quality of a star performer.
informal
exceptionally good; outstanding.
symposium
( n.) reference to the celebrations in ancient Greece; specifically, the part of a banquet that takes place after a meal the meal, when drinking for pleasure is accompanied by music dancing ,recitals, or conversation.
The Greek symposium was a key Hellenic social institution.It was a forum for men to debate,plot boast, or simply revel with others. The symposium was held to celebrate special occasions ,such as athletic victories and poetic contests.The symposium was a source of pride for all.

OUR PURPOSE:

  • To encourage people to engage with each other in an environment that is both intellectual and recreational.
  • To replicate, albeit with a much more modern twist, the symposium atmosphere of Ancient Greece and Rome, when men like Plato would debate, discuss philosophy, and share ideas.

What You Can Expect From Us:

  • Comfortable stylish seating, an upscale experience,where people can exchange ideas, that encourages an open mind and open heart, in a fun, stress free space.
  • A cool place to "hang out", eat, drink, or just sit back, observe and relax.
  • Activities: Play darts, dance, listen to music. We bring in art exhibits, live musicians, and guest speakers too! We even will take suggestions for future monthly features.

The Stellar Symposium ...a new way to unwind.
